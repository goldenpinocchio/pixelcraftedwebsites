FAQ

Common questions

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects are completed in 2–4 weeks. Simpler one-page sites can be done in as little as 1–2 weeks. Larger or more complex projects may take longer. I'll give you a clear timeline upfront.

Do I own my website after it's built?

100%. You own all design files, code, and content. If you cancel hosting, I can help you migrate everything to a new host. Your website is yours forever.

What happens after the first year of hosting?

Hosting is $120/year after the first year. You're free to cancel anytime — I'll help you export everything. No contracts, no lock-in.

Can you work with businesses outside of Columbus?

Absolutely. Columbus is home base, but I work with businesses all over the country. The process works the same — video calls, messaging, email. Distance isn't a barrier.

What does the free marketing consulting include?

I cover the basics: Google Business Profile setup and optimization, SEO recommendations specific to your industry, content ideas, and general digital strategy. It's not a full marketing agency — but it's real, actionable advice from someone who builds sites for a living.
